Meet The Experts

This is the team that take any idea and turn it into a reality

Zakaria Bell

Principal

Zakaria leads his practice with thoughtful investigation; asking expansive questions that invite the abounding possibilities for playful and provocative spaces, balancing both form and function. For Zakaria, building considerate architecture begins with an insightful read of the relationship between people and place. In particular, he advocates for adaptive reuse and environmentally responsible architecture.
